My Hero – My Father

CHM Amar Jeet Singh

My father, CHM Amar Jeet Singh, is currently serving in the Kupwara district of Jammu & Kashmir — one of the most challenging and critical regions of our country. As a senior leader in his unit, his role carries immense responsibility and honor.

His strength, discipline, and selfless service have always inspired me. He is not just my father — he is my role model, my pride, and my hero. This website is my humble tribute to him and to every brave soul who wears the uniform with pride. Jai Hind
